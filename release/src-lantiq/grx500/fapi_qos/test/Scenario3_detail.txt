Three streams S1, S2, S3 from LAN to WAN.

Stream 1 : UDP traffic with destination port 1024
Stream 2 : Traffic with destination IP address 10.10.10.1
Stream 3 : Traffic with destination MAC address 00:1A:BC:40:EF:80
